Lee Chang-Hsin

Biography

Lee Chang-Hsin was born in Taiwan, lived in Paris (France) from 1985 to 2018, then moved to Montréal (Canada) in 2019

Studies

1988-1992: Ecole nationale supérieure des Arts Décoratifs (Paris, France) Art and space Department, ENSAD Diploma.
1979-1984: Fine Arts University (Taipei, Taiwan) / Master's Degree.

Art Practice

1992-Now: Artist (sculptor, painter)
2003-2012: Visual Arts consultant for "Counterpoint Ensemble" Company
1997-2001: Composition Teacher ("Jin-Wen University", Taipei, Taiwan)

Exhibitions
